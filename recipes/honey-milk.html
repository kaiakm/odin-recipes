Ponyo's Honey Milk

Description

This honestly looks so good and I want to make it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • cinnamon to sprinkle on top

Instructions

  1. In a small pot on a stove or in a microwave, heat the milk until steaming but not boiling.
  2. Pour the hot milk into a mug.
  3. Sprinkle on cinnamon or any other add-ons you want to include.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
