Totoro Bento

Description

The lunch that Satsuki prepares for both her sister Mei and herself in My Neighbor Totoro.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 umeboshi
  • 1 shishamo
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • handful frozen edamame
  • 1 tbsp sakura denbu

Steps

  1. Put cooked rice in your bento box about 3-4 of the way full (leave some room at the top for your topping and to be able to put the lid on).
  2. Take 1 umeboshi and put it on one side of the rice.
  3. In a small pan, heat the oil on medium-high. Cook one shishamo on both sides until cooked.
  4. Place the cooked shishamo across the middle of the bento box on the rice.
  5. Boil water in a small pot. Put ina handful of frozen edamame and cook according to package instructions.
  6. Once cooked, pop the edamame beans out of the pod. Arrange them on the rice to fill up a little less than one quarter of the bento box, under the shishamo.
  7. Sprinkle the sakura denby on the rice next to the edamame.
  8. Enjoy whenever it's lunch time! Put a lid on it and pack it away to take it with you.
